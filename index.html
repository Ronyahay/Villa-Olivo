About the Villa

A Sanctuary Above the Aegean

Nestled in the tranquil village of Kampani on the Akrotiri peninsula, Villa Olivo is a rare jewel — a spacious, fully-accessible luxury retreat where every detail has been thoughtfully crafted for your comfort and pleasure.

Surrounded by ancient olive trees with sea views stretching to the horizon, the villa offers absolute privacy and an atmosphere of timeless Cretan elegance. From the illuminated pool on summer nights to the sun-drenched terraces by day, every moment here is extraordinary.

Rated 10/10 on Booking.com and 4.9★ on Airbnb, our guests return year after year, drawn by the exceptional hospitality and unmatched setting.

Inclusive by Design

Everyone Is Welcome Here

Villa Olivo was thoughtfully designed so that every guest — regardless of mobility — can enjoy the full luxury experience in complete comfort and independence.

🛗

Private Elevator

A dedicated in-villa elevator connects all floors, ensuring seamless and effortless access throughout the entire property — no stairs required.

♿

Step-Free Access

Wide doorways, smooth level thresholds, and ramped entrances allow wheelchair and mobility aid users to move freely in every area of the villa.

🏊

Accessible Pool Entry

The private swimming pool features a specially designed accessible entry system, so all guests — including those with limited mobility — can enjoy a refreshing dip.

🚿

Walk-in Showers

Bathrooms are equipped with barrier-free walk-in showers with grab rails, providing safe and comfortable bathing for everyone.

🅿️

Accessible Parking

Dedicated accessible parking spaces are available directly at the villa entrance, making arrival and departure effortless.

🌿

Flat Garden Paths

The landscaped gardens feature smooth, level pathways so guests can enjoy the olive groves, sun terrace, and outdoor spaces without barriers.
